About Me

Why I decided to code

I've always had an interest in technology. In 2017, I graduated from UW Foster School of Business with BA in Business Administration with a double focus in Information Systems and Operations & Supply Chain Management.


After graduating, I taught English in Spain for two years. Upon returning, I had a bit of a difficult time deciding what to pursue next, but I knew that it would be in the world of tech. After doing some research, and getting a recommendation, I found myself applying to Epicodus.

The rest is future.
